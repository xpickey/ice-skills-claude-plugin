## IFRS9 & Risk Management

### IFRS9 Expected Credit Loss (ECL)
- **3 Stages**: Stage 1 (12-month ECL), Stage 2 (Lifetime ECL — SICR), Stage 3 (Credit-impaired)
- **Components**: PD (Probability of Default), LGD (Loss Given Default), EAD (Exposure at Default)
- **Forward-looking**: Macroeconomic scenarios, overlay adjustments
- **Implementation**: ECL Engine architecture, data requirements, reconciliation

### Risk Management
- Credit Risk: portfolio assessment, concentration risk, stress testing
- Operational Risk: process controls, fraud prevention
- Model Risk Management: model validation, governance
