## Archetype classification

Plot MEDDPICC subtotal against Why-Stack subtotal:
- [ ] 🟢 **Aligned Believer** — MEDDPICC ≥ 30 **and** Why-Stack ≥ 18
- [ ] 🟡 **Technical Fit** — MEDDPICC ≥ 30 **but** Why-Stack < 18 → **price-war risk**
- [ ] 🟡 **Spiritual Connection** — MEDDPICC < 30 **but** Why-Stack ≥ 18 → **will stall**
- [ ] 🔴 **Lost Cause** — both below the thresholds → **disqualify**
