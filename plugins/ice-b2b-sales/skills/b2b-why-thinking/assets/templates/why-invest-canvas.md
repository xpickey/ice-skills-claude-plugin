## Synthesized Why Invest paragraph (paste into proposal)

> The investment of **[X] Million Baht** is not about cost — it is about **[shared belief]**.
>
> **Financially**, it returns **[Y]%** over 3 years and breaks even at **[Z] months**.
> **Strategically**, it prevents **[competitive risk]** worth **[K] Million** in lost margin.
> **Personally**, it positions **[EB team]** as the group that **[outcome]**.
>
> The question is not "can we afford this?" — it is "can we afford **not** to?"

---

## Red flags (fix before shipping)

- [ ] "Budget is tight" heard more than once → Lens 1 not sharp enough.
- [ ] No Lens 2 argument → proposal will lose the board.
- [ ] EB hasn't mentioned this initiative internally → Lens 3 is weak.
- [ ] All numbers are in the competitor's range → build the *non-financial* case harder.
