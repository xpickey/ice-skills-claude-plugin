## Quality gates (all must pass before use)

- [ ] Remove our name — no competitor can paste theirs in and keep it.
- [ ] Contains no KPI / financial number.
- [ ] Contains no product / service name.
- [ ] Two sentences or fewer.
- [ ] A 10-year-old gets it.
- [ ] Current customers would say "yes, that's why I picked you."
- [ ] Will still be valid in 10 years.
