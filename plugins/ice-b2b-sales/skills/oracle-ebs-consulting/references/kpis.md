## PART 14: Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Financial KPIs

**General Ledger**:
- Days to close (target: 5-10 days)
- Number of manual journal entries (target: minimize)
- Percentage of auto-posted journals (target: >90%)
- Account reconciliation completion rate (target: 100% by close)

**Accounts Payable**:
- Invoice processing time (target: <3 days from receipt to approval)
- Straight-through processing rate (target: >60% no-touch processing)
- Early payment discount capture (target: >80% of available discounts)
- Supplier payment terms compliance (target: >95% on-time payments)
- Average days payable outstanding (DPO): Industry benchmark 45-60 days

**Accounts Receivable**:
- Days Sales Outstanding (DSO): Target <45 days
- Collection effectiveness index (CEI): Target >90%
- Bad debt write-offs as % of revenue: Target <1%
- Customer invoice accuracy: Target >99%
- Percentage of invoices disputed: Target <5%

### Procurement KPIs

**Purchasing**:
- PO cycle time (requisition to PO): Target <2 days
- PO compliance rate (purchases with PO): Target >95%
- Supplier on-time delivery: Target >90%
- Receipt accuracy: Target >98%
- Cost savings from strategic sourcing: Target 5-10% annually

**Inventory**:
- Inventory turnover: Target 6-12x per year (varies by industry)
- Inventory accuracy: Target >95%
- Stockout frequency: Target <5% of time
- Excess and obsolete inventory: Target <5% of total inventory value
- Carrying cost as % of inventory value: Benchmark 20-30%

### Manufacturing KPIs

**Production**:
- On-time delivery: Target >90%
- Manufacturing cycle time: Minimize (lead time reduction)
- Capacity utilization: Target 75-85% (allows for flexibility)
- First-pass yield: Target >95%
- Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE): Target >85%

**Quality**:
- Defect rate: Target <1%
- Customer return rate: Target <0.5%
- Scrap and rework cost: Target <2% of COGS
- Quality audit score: Target >90%

### HR & Payroll KPIs

**Payroll**:
- Payroll processing time: Target <2 days
- Payroll accuracy: Target >99.5%
- Employee self-service adoption: Target >80%
- Time-to-resolve payroll queries: Target <48 hours

**HR**:
- Time-to-hire: Target <30 days
- Employee turnover rate: Industry average 10-15% (lower is better)
- Training completion rate: Target >90%
- Employee satisfaction score: Target >75%
