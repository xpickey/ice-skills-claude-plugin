## PART 5: Oracle EBS HRMS / HCM

### Core HR

**Organization Setup**:
- **Business Group**: Top-level org, legal entity, country, legislation
- **Legal Entity**: Company registration, tax ID, legal address
- **Operating Unit**: Cost center, profit center, business unit
- **Organization Hierarchy**: Reporting structure, management hierarchy, position hierarchy
- **Location**: Office locations, addresses, work locations

**Employee Management**:
- **Person Types**: Employee, contingent worker, applicant, ex-employee
- **Hire Employee**: Hire date, position, grade, location, manager, employment terms
- **Assignment**: Primary assignment, secondary assignment, global assignment
- **Employee Number**: Manual, automatic, number format, uniqueness
- **Personal Information**: Name, date of birth, national ID, address, contacts, emergency contact
- **Job & Position**: Job definition, position, grade, pay scale, competencies
- **Workforce Structures**: Department, job, position, grade, pay scale

**HR Transactions**:
- **Promotion**: New grade, new position, new salary, effective date
- **Transfer**: Change department, change location, change manager, change job
- **Termination**: Resignation, retirement, dismissal, termination reason, final settlement
- **Salary Change**: Merit increase, promotion increase, market adjustment, effective date
- **Work Schedule**: Work hours, shift pattern, working days, overtime eligibility
- **Leave of Absence**: Unpaid leave, maternity leave, sick leave, leave without pay

**Employee Self-Service (ESS)**:
- **Personal Information**: Update address, phone, emergency contact, banking details
- **View Payslip**: Access payslips, year-to-date earnings, tax deductions
- **Time Entry**: Enter time, submit time, view time approval status
- **Leave Request**: Request leave, view leave balance, cancel leave request
- **Benefits Enrollment**: Enroll in benefits, change beneficiaries, life events

### Payroll

**Payroll Setup**:
- **Payroll Definition**: Payroll name, frequency (monthly, semi-monthly, weekly), period calendar
- **Consolidation Set**: Group payrolls for consolidated processing
- **Element Classifications**: Earnings, pre-tax deductions, post-tax deductions, employer charges
- **Payroll Period**: Period start, period end, payment date, cut-off date
- **Payroll Parameters**: Default costing, default balancing, tax calculation rules

**Elements & Formulas**:
- **Element Definition**: Element name, classification, processing type (recurring, non-recurring)
- **Element Input Values**: Amount, rate, units, start date, end date
- **Element Links**: Link to organization, link to payroll, link to grade, eligibility rules
- **Element Balances**: YTD balances, QTD balances, MTD balances, pay period balances
- **Fast Formulas**: Formula editor, calculation logic, conditional logic, user-defined functions
- **Pre-Payments**: Advances, loans, loan deductions, balloon payments

**Payroll Processing**:
- **Payroll Run**: QuickPay (single employee), Payroll run (all employees), retry, rollback
- **Run Results**: Gross pay, deductions, net pay, employer charges
- **Pre-Payroll Process**: Import time, calculate absences, process elements
- **Post-Payroll Process**: Transfer to GL, payment processing, archive
- **Payroll Costing**: Cost allocation to departments, projects, cost centers
- **Payslip**: Generate payslips, email payslips, print payslips, ESS access

**Tax & Statutory Reporting**:
- **Income Tax**: Tax calculation, tax brackets, allowances, exemptions, tax withholding
- **Social Security**: Employee contribution, employer contribution, wage ceiling
- **Provident Fund**: Employee contribution %, employer contribution %, voluntary contribution
- **Tax Filing**: Personal Income Tax (PND 1, PND 1 Kor), Corporate Income Tax
- **Year-End Processing**: W-2 (US), P11D (UK), Annual Tax Statement (Thailand)
- **Regulatory Reports**: Social Security Fund, Provident Fund, Workmen's Compensation

### Time and Labor

**Time Entry**:
- **Time Card**: Daily time entry, weekly time entry, project time, overtime entry
- **Time Approval**: Manager approval, timekeeper approval, project manager approval
- **Time Rules**: Overtime rules, shift differential, premium pay, rounding rules
- **Absence Management**: Leave types, leave accrual, leave balance, leave approval

**Attendance**:
- **Attendance Tracking**: Clock in/out, time clock integration, biometric integration
- **Shift Management**: Shift patterns, rotating shifts, shift swaps
- **Attendance Exceptions**: Late arrival, early departure, missing punch, unauthorized absence

### Compensation & Benefits

**Compensation Management**:
- **Salary Basis**: Annual salary, hourly rate, daily rate, piece rate
- **Salary Structures**: Pay scale, grade, salary range, midpoint, compa-ratio
- **Salary Proposal**: Merit increase budget, promotion budget, market adjustment
- **Variable Pay**: Bonus, commission, incentive pay, performance-based pay
- **Total Compensation Statement**: Base salary, bonus, benefits value, total rewards

**Benefits Administration**:
- **Benefits Plans**: Health insurance, life insurance, dental, vision, retirement
- **Plan Design**: Employer contribution, employee contribution, coverage tiers
- **Enrollment**: Open enrollment, new hire enrollment, life event enrollment
- **Benefits Costing**: Premium calculation, employer cost, employee cost, GL posting
- **Benefits Reconciliation**: Carrier billing, premium reconciliation, variance resolution

**Performance Management**:
- **Goal Setting**: SMART goals, cascading goals, goal alignment
- **Performance Review**: Mid-year review, annual review, 360-degree feedback
- **Rating Scales**: Rating model, rating scales, forced distribution
- **Compensation Linkage**: Performance-based pay, merit increase matrix
