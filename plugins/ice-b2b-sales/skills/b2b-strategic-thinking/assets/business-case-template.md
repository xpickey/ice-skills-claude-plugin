# Business Case Template (CFO-grade)

A defensible business case for B2B enterprise software. Built on the Value Engineering Logic Tree: Revenue ↑ + Cost ↓ + Cash ↑ + Risk ↓ → driver → metric → lever → capability → proof.

This template assumes a decision-maker at CFO altitude. For CIO, swap "cash impact by month" emphasis for "architecture / risk / lifecycle"; for owner-led mid-market, lead with monthly cash-out / cash-in.
