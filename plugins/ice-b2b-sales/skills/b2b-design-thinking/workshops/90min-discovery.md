# 90-Minute Discovery Workshop Kit

A compact, C-suite-grade discovery workshop. Use at the first executive touchpoint when there is not time for a half-day, or as the opening move of a larger engagement. Discipline: **DT-primary with ST bookends**.

## Objective

Leave the room with:
- A shared understanding of the client's top pain and aspiration
- A prioritized short list of How-Might-We questions
- One named next step

## Ideal Audience

3–6 senior stakeholders — typically Economic Buyer, Technical Buyer, and 1–2 user champions. Optional: a sponsor / Coach who briefs us beforehand.

## Pre-Workshop (1 hour of prep)

- Strategic fit check: is this account on our target list?
- Stakeholder intelligence: recent earnings call / news / LinkedIn scans
- Agenda pre-share (1-page, 3 days prior)
- Room setup / Miro board ready
- Name a facilitator (you) and a note-taker (colleague)

## Agenda (90 minutes)

### 0:00–0:10 — Open (10 min)
- Welcome, introductions (2 min per person)
- Confirm outcomes: *"By 10:30, we want to agree on top 3 HMWs and one next step"*
- Hopes & fears (2 min silent + 3 min share)

### 0:10–0:25 — Context share (15 min)
- Exec reaction to a 3-slide mini briefing: market pressures, peer benchmarks, our take
- Tone: we listen more than we present
- Capture reactions on sticky notes

### 0:25–0:50 — Stakeholder map + AS-IS journey (25 min)
- Plenary co-creation on whiteboard / Miro
- Ask: *"Walk me through what happens today when [core process]"*
- Mark pain points, moments of truth, stuck stages
- Everyone sees the same picture form in real time

### 0:50–1:05 — HMW generation (15 min)
- From journey pain, draft 5–8 HMWs
- *"How might we [verb] [persona] [outcome]?"*
- 5 min silent, 10 min shared

### 1:05–1:25 — Prioritization + next step (20 min)
- Dot voting (3 dots each) on HMW shortlist
- Discuss top 3
- Agree: what would each party do to explore further?
- Commit a date for the next conversation

### 1:25–1:30 — Close (5 min)
- Recap the three HMWs, the next step, the owners
- Thank stakeholders
- Send photo / screenshot of boards within 24 hours

## Deliverables (within 48 hours)

- Workshop report (2 pages): attendees, context, journey, HMWs, next steps
- Cleaned-up journey map
- Top 3 HMWs formatted for the proposal / next workshop
- CRM updates: account stage, stakeholders mapped, next activity dated

## Thai Adaptation

- Add 5 min relationship time at the start (tea / coffee, small talk)
- Use silent sticky notes for HMW generation (not verbal brainstorm)
- Honor seniority in introductions — senior stakeholder first
- For pain points, frame as "the process fails" not "someone fails"
- If the senior is cautious, close with *"we will follow up after your internal consultation"* rather than forcing a commitment

## Materials Kit

- 3-slide mini briefing (printed + digital)
- Large whiteboard or Miro template (journey, HMW)
- Sticky notes, Sharpies, dot stickers
- Timer visible to all
- Note-taker's template (structured, auto-populates report)

## Facilitator Self-Check

- [ ] Strategic fit confirmed before the meeting
- [ ] Stakeholder intelligence synthesized
- [ ] Agenda shared 3 days prior
- [ ] Room / Miro ready
- [ ] Note-taker briefed
- [ ] 48-hour deliverable plan agreed
- [ ] Thai adaptations applied if audience is Thai-speaking

## Common Failure Modes

- **Facilitator presents instead of listens** — the mini-briefing is 5 min, not 45
- **No prioritization** — ends with 15 HMWs and no focus
- **No next step** — ends with "we'll get back to you" (never happens)
- **Junior voices unheard** — use silent sticky method to equalize
