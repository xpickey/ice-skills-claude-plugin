# 1-Day Ideation Workshop Kit

A full-day (7–8 hour) session to take a customer from discovery through ideation to a validated top-3 concept set with a draft value case. Discipline: **DT-primary with ST fit gate and value case**.

## Objective

Leave the day with:
- Robust AS-IS journey for the core process in scope
- 5 prioritized HMWs
- 10+ ideated concepts; top 3 storyboarded
- Draft Why-Invest / Why-Now / Why-Us
- Strategic fit confirmed or adjusted
- 30-60-90 plan with named owners

## Ideal Audience

10–15 cross-functional participants. Executive sponsor present at open + close; working team present throughout.

## Pre-Workshop (1–2 days of prep)

- Strategic fit check + account strategy review
- 1-on-1 pre-interviews with 3–5 key attendees (30 min each)
- Pre-read (3 pages) sent 1 week prior
- Pre-work: each attendee maps their piece of the AS-IS journey
- Room setup: U-shape + breakout tables + wall space
- Miro / Mural / FigJam master board pre-built
- Facilitator + 2 co-facilitators + SME bench

## Agenda (8 hours including lunch)

### 0:00–0:30 — Open (30 min)
- Executive sponsor welcome (10 min)
- Agenda walk-through (5 min)
- Introductions + hopes/fears (10 min)
- Working agreements (5 min)

### 0:30–2:00 — Discovery block (90 min)
- Persona confirmation (15 min)
- Stakeholder map (15 min)
- AS-IS journey — plenary co-creation (45 min)
- Moments of truth + pain prioritization (15 min)

### 2:00–3:00 — Problem framing (60 min)
- POV statements (20 min)
- HMW generation (20 min)
- HMW prioritization — impact × feasibility dot vote (20 min)

### 3:00–4:00 — Lunch (60 min)
- Informal relationship time (Thai context: critical)
- Facilitator quiet prep for afternoon

### 4:00–5:30 — Ideation block (90 min)
Against each of the top 3 HMWs:
- Crazy 8s (30 min)
- Analogous inspiration (20 min)
- Tech-forward: AI, low-code, agentic (20 min)
- Share and cluster (20 min)

### 5:30–6:30 — Convergence (60 min)
- Impact-effort matrix (20 min)
- Top 3 concepts — 6-panel storyboards (30 min in 3 groups)
- Gallery walk + critique (10 min)

### 6:30–7:00 — Value case draft (30 min)
- Why-Invest / Why-Now / Why-Us for the top concept
- Quantify opportunity where possible
- Flag assumptions for later validation

### 7:00–7:20 — Close (20 min)
- Executive sponsor re-enters
- 3 concepts presented (3 min each)
- Commitments for 30-60-90 days
- Thank you

## Deliverables (within 7 business days)

- Full workshop report (15–20 pages)
- Updated journey maps (AS-IS and TO-BE draft)
- POV / HMW register
- Impact-effort matrix with ranked ideas
- Storyboards (3)
- Why-Invest / Why-Now / Why-Us one-pager
- 30-60-90 plan with owners and dates
- Updated account strategy
- Proposal draft (if appropriate)

## Strategic Fit Gate (mid-afternoon)

At lunch / break transition, the facilitator pauses with the sponsor:
- Do the emerging concepts still fit our Where-to-Play?
- Do pricing / commercial expectations fit our envelope?
- Any red flags suggesting we should reshape ambition or decline?

This is the single most important moment to protect against DT pulling the firm into the wrong deal.

## Thai Adaptation

- 30-minute opening with refreshments (beyond the facilitated 20 min)
- Silent + written ideation minimum 40% of ideation time
- Small-group breakouts for junior voices
- Plenary only for framing / decision
- Lunch is relationship-building, not just fuel — treat as part of the workshop
- Language mix: Thai emotional / empathy, English analytical / framework
- Closing: commitments can be "subject to internal consultation" — honor this

## Materials Kit

- Pre-read + pre-work instructions (1 week prior)
- Printed agenda per seat
- Named place cards (honor hierarchy)
- Journey map canvas (A0 printed or Miro)
- POV / HMW template sheets
- Crazy 8s paper (2 per person for multiple rounds)
- Storyboard sheets (6-panel, 3 per group)
- Impact-effort matrix poster
- Why-I/N/U canvas
- Sharpies, dot stickers, timers, tape
- Camera / screenshot owner

## Facilitator Self-Check

- [ ] 1-on-1 pre-interviews complete
- [ ] Pre-read + pre-work distributed and acknowledged
- [ ] Strategic fit gate scheduled
- [ ] SME bench ready for questions
- [ ] Executive sponsor's time blocked for open + close
- [ ] 7-day deliverable owner named
- [ ] Thai adaptations applied if audience is Thai

## Common Failure Modes

- **No strategic fit gate** — enthusiasm drags the firm into the wrong deal
- **Ideation rushed** — 90 min is the floor; do not cut
- **Storyboards skipped** — concepts stay abstract
- **Executive absent** — without sponsor at open + close, commitments evaporate
- **Deliverable slips** — 7 days is already generous; if slip happens, send interim update
