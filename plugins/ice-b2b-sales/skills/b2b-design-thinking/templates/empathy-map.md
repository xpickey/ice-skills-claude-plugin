# Empathy Map Template

A one-page canvas to capture what a specific user (persona or named individual) sees, hears, thinks, feels, says, and does in relation to the problem in scope. Use at the Empathize phase of Design Thinking.

## When to Use
- Kicking off discovery for a new buyer persona (CFO, CIO, LOB leader, end user)
- Synthesizing 3–5 empathy interviews into a single artifact
- Preparing a workshop where personas must be shared quickly
- Framing pain and gain before POV / HMW generation

## Canvas Structure

```
+-------------------------------------------------------------+
|  Persona: [name / role / archetype]                         |
|  Scenario: [what is happening when we observe them?]        |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------+
|  SEES                |  HEARS                               |
|  What is in their    |  What do peers, bosses, media say?   |
|  environment?        |                                      |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------+
|  THINKS & FEELS                                             |
|  What really matters? Worries? Aspirations?                 |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------+
|  SAYS & DOES                                                |
|  Public statements, behaviors observed                      |
+----------------------+--------------------------------------+
|  PAINS                       |  GAINS                       |
|  Fears, obstacles, frustra-  |  Wants, measures of success, |
|  tions                       |  aspirations                 |
+------------------------------+------------------------------+
```

## Guiding Questions

**Sees** — What does their dashboard look like? Which reports land on their desk? Who walks into their office?

**Hears** — What does the CEO say in town halls? What do analysts say about the industry? What do peers in their network complain about?

**Thinks & Feels** — What keeps them up at night? What would make them a hero? What are they embarrassed to admit?

**Says & Does** — What do they say in earnings calls / town halls / internal memos? What behaviors do colleagues observe?

**Pains** — What is blocking them right now? What have they tried that did not work?

**Gains** — What would "done" look like? How would they be measured?

## Example — Thai Manufacturing CFO

- Scenario: Planning FY27 budget; ECC SAP sunset looming; BOI audit pending
- Sees: monthly close still takes 12 days, 3 subsidiaries on different ledgers
- Hears: auditors asking about IFRS 9 readiness; peers at CPF and SCG moving to S/4HANA
- Thinks & Feels: worried about audit finding; anxious about justifying capex
- Says & Does: asking IT for "a solid plan", deferring big decisions
- Pains: cannot close faster without more headcount; fears vendor lock-in
- Gains: 5-day close, clean audit, board confidence, BOI defensibility

## Quality Bar

- Based on real interviews or observations (not invented)
- Specific verbs and nouns, not generic
- Contradictions surfaced, not smoothed over
- Links to at least two POV statements and three HMW questions
