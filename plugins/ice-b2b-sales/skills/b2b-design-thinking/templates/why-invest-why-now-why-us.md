## Assembled Narrative (paste into proposal)

Use this template sentence to braid the three answers into one story:

> *"[Client] has until [specific date] to address [regulatory / competitive / contractual pressure]. Doing nothing costs [quantified risk or opportunity]. The solution unlocks [specific value outcomes]. [Our firm] is uniquely positioned to deliver this because of [key differentiator 1], [differentiator 2], and [differentiator 3], with [named reference] to prove it, and we commit to [specific outcome / SLA]."*

## Output Formats

### Three-column one-slide
Single slide, three columns labeled "Why Invest | Why Now | Why Us". Each column has 3–4 bullets.

### Three-paragraph narrative
For proposal body. One paragraph per question, 3–5 sentences each.

### Executive memo
Situation → Complication → Question → Answer → three grouped reasons → next action.

### Sales talk-track (5 minutes)
- Opening (30 sec): *"The reason we are recommending this now is…"*
- Why Invest (60 sec)
- Why Now (60 sec)
- Why Us (60 sec)
- Close (30 sec): ask for the specific next step

## Self-Check

- [ ] Cost of inaction is quantified or concrete
- [ ] Timing trigger is specific and dated
- [ ] Each differentiator is concrete (not generic)
- [ ] The narrative reads as one story
- [ ] A named next action is present
- [ ] Language matches audience seniority
- [ ] For Thai audiences: local triggers and references used
- [ ] Nothing generic (no "digital transformation", "AI-powered", "innovative")
