# Pink Sunrise (gradient)

- **Family**: warm
- **Start**: #f857a6  |  **End**: #ff5858
- **5-stop ramp**: #f857a6, #fa5792, #fc587f, #fd586c, #ff5858
- **CSS**: `linear-gradient(135deg, #f857a6 0%, #ff5858 100%)`
- **Best for**: Campaign/launch; vibrant

## How to use in a deck

Use as a title/section background or as an accent bar. For body slides keep it subtle (low opacity or a thin top strip) so text stays readable. Pair with white or near-black text depending on the stop luminance.
