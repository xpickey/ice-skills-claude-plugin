# Question Bank (125 curated questions)

Questions are indexed by stage of the enterprise software lifecycle and tagged with framework origin, target persona, and purpose. When the user asks for examples, a starter set, or inspiration, select 8–20 questions that fit their specific inputs — do not dump the full bank.

## 1. Prospecting (10)

1. *I noticed [public signal — earnings call, regulatory filing, leadership change]; is [inferred priority] a live theme on your roadmap this year?* — Situation / AE → CxO.
2. *What has changed in your finance or operations priorities in the last six months that we should understand?* — Situation / AE → Economic Buyer.
3. *Is there an active transformation program you are sponsoring right now, or is this earlier-stage thinking?* — Qualification / AE → Sponsor.
4. *Who else in your leadership team is feeling the same pressure around [domain]?* — Champion-identification / AE → Contact.
5. *Would it be helpful if I shared a one-pager on how three peer organizations handled this before we invest a meeting?* — Reciprocity / AE → Prospect.
6. *What would have to be true for this conversation to be worth thirty minutes of your time?* — Qualifying / AE → Senior prospect.
7. *How are you measuring success in your current role this year — what are the top two or three numbers on your scorecard?* — Metrics / AE → Economic Buyer.
8. *When you look across your current vendor landscape, what do you wish you had more of and less of?* — Open / AE → Technical Buyer.
9. *If I introduced you to a peer who solved this last year, would you find that useful?* — Trust-building / AE → Prospect.
10. *Is this conversation something you would prefer to continue with me alone, or is there a colleague you would want in the room?* — Committee surface / AE → Prospect.

## 2. Discovery — business context (10)

11. *Walk me through the last twelve months from your desk — what has gone well, what has been harder than expected?* — SPIN-Situation / AE → Economic Buyer.
12. *How does your organization define success at the business-unit level, and how does that cascade into your team's objectives?* — Strategic Selling / AE → CxO.
13. *Where does your current finance/operations/HR function sit on a maturity curve, in your own assessment?* — Socratic / AE → CxO.
14. *Which three processes, if they ran materially better, would move the P&L most?* — Prioritization / AE → CFO.
15. *Who are the two or three people whose daily work would change most if this transformation succeeded?* — User Buyer identification / AE → Champion.
16. *What have you already tried to fix [problem], and what happened?* — Sandler-L3 / AE → Operational Sponsor.
17. *Where is the gap between what the Board sees on paper and what your team sees day to day?* — Socratic / AE → CFO.
18. *If you had to describe your current platform architecture in one sentence, what would it be?* — Situation / AE → CIO.
19. *How has this conversation gone before with other vendors — what did they miss?* — Competitive / AE → Champion.
20. *What is the single question your CEO has been asking you that you have been struggling to answer?* — Challenger / AE → CFO or COO.

## 3. Discovery — process deep-dive (15)

21. *Walk me through the month-end close from day one to close-of-close — where does the time actually go?* — JTBD / SC → Finance Director.
22. *At which step in the close does the team work the longest hours?* — Specific / SC → Controller.
23. *When a reconciliation breaks, what is the first thing that happens and how long does it take to resolve?* — Process / SC → SME.
24. *Which reports do you produce today that no one actually reads?* — Provocative / SC → Finance Director.
25. *Which reports do people ask for that you cannot produce today?* — JTBD / SC → Finance Director.
26. *Where do the Finance and Procurement data sets disagree most often, and what is the typical reconciliation path?* — Process / SC → SMEs.
27. *If a user wanted to know the status of a PO in real time today, what would they have to do?* — User-journey / SC → SME.
28. *Which parts of the current process exist only because of regulatory requirement, and which exist because of habit?* — Socratic / SC → Finance/Compliance.
29. *Which month-end controls are in place because of the auditor, and which were designed by the team for the team?* — Socratic / SC → Audit/Finance.
30. *Where in the procure-to-pay cycle do you have the most maverick spend?* — Specific / SC → Procurement Head.
31. *How does a new hire get added to the system today from first day to productive day?* — JTBD / SC → HR/IT.
32. *If we redesigned one process end-to-end, which one would unlock the others?* — Prioritization / SC → CFO or COO.
33. *What data exists today that nobody is using, and what data do you wish existed?* — JTBD / SC → Analytics Lead.
34. *If the Board asked you tomorrow for a forward-looking cash forecast, how would you produce it today?* — Specific / SC → Treasury.
35. *Where in your current process is there an Excel spreadsheet that is critical and fragile?* — Specific / SC → Operations.

## 4. Pain, Implication, and quantification (15)

36. *What is the business cost today of the ten-day close — in people, in working capital, in risk?* — SPIN-Implication / AE → CFO.
37. *If the close stays at ten days for another year, what decisions get made slower than they should?* — SPIN-Implication / AE → CFO.
38. *If your auditor downgraded the assessment of your controls, what would that cost you with lenders or regulators?* — Challenger-Implication / AE → CFO.
39. *How many finance-team resignations last year were influenced by workload during month-end?* — Quantification / AE → CFO or HR Head.
40. *What opportunity cost are you carrying because your team is cleaning data instead of analyzing it?* — SPIN-Implication / AE → CFO.
41. *If the ERP stays on-premise for three more years, what does that do to your cloud strategy and your cost base?* — SPIN-Implication / AE → CIO.
42. *What has a competitor done recently that made you wish you had better systems?* — Challenger / AE → COO or CEO.
43. *If the same procurement cycle time persists, what does it do to supplier goodwill and to price leverage?* — SPIN-Implication / AE → CPO.
44. *What has this problem already cost you that you cannot recover — and what is it projected to cost in the next twelve months?* — Sandler-L5 / AE → CFO.
45. *How does the current pain show up in your own workweek, personally?* — Sandler-L6 / AE → Sponsor.
46. *If you had to name the moment in the year when this problem hurts most, when is it?* — Specificity / AE → Sponsor.
47. *What does the CEO say when the problem surfaces in an Exec meeting?* — Political-weight / AE → Sponsor.
48. *How many Board-level escalations has this issue triggered in the last twelve months?* — Quantification / AE → CFO.
49. *What happens to your risk-rating from your banks if the finance function does not modernize in the next two years?* — Challenger / AE → CFO.
50. *If nothing changes, what is the worst realistic outcome you can imagine two years from now?* — Socratic-Implication / AE → Sponsor.

## 5. MEDDPICC probes (12)

51. *What are the three quantified outcomes you will use to measure success twelve months after go-live?* — M / AE → Economic Buyer.
52. *Besides yourself, who has the authority to commit budget at this level?* — E / AE → Champion.
53. *If the CEO asked tomorrow who owns this decision, what would you say?* — E / AE → Champion.
54. *What are the three criteria that will matter most when you compare vendors?* — DC / AE → Committee member.
55. *Of those three criteria, which one is most likely to be the deciding factor?* — DC / AE → Sponsor.
56. *Walk me through the decision process — who evaluates, who recommends, who approves, and on what calendar?* — DP / AE → Procurement or Champion.
57. *Which committee signatures are required before a contract can be executed?* — PP / AE → Procurement.
58. *Are there any e-bidding, government, or audit requirements that govern this procurement?* — PP / AE → Procurement.
59. *If you had to rank the pain points in order of urgency, how would you rank them?* — I / AE → Sponsor.
60. *What would have to happen for this project to be postponed to next fiscal year?* — Deal risk / AE → Champion.
61. *Who else are you evaluating, and what draws you to them?* — Comp / AE → Champion (asked carefully).
62. *If we were not in the picture, which option would you pick and why?* — Comp-Challenger variant / AE → Champion.

## 6. Solution design workshop (15)

63. *If we were designing this process on a clean sheet of paper, what would you want it to look like?* — Design-Thinking / SC → Process owner.
64. *How might we compress the close to four days without adding finance headcount?* — HMW / SC → Finance team.
65. *Which of your current processes do you want to preserve, and which are you willing to let go?* — Change readiness / SC → Sponsor.
66. *If we had to eliminate three current reports to earn permission to add three new ones, which three would you retire?* — Scope / SC → Finance Director.
67. *Where do you expect your business to be different in three years, and what does that mean for the design?* — Future-state / SC → CxO.
68. *What is the one design decision that, if wrong, would sink the project?* — Risk / SC → Sponsor.
69. *Which design choices here are reversible, and which are one-way doors?* — Socratic / SC → CIO.
70. *What are we designing for that is unique to Thailand, and what is unique to your company?* — Localization / SC → Finance/Tax.
71. *Which of your current customizations are truly required, and which were built because no one challenged them?* — Socratic / SC → IT Head.
72. *If a new finance leader joins two years from now, would they find this design intuitive?* — Future-proofing / SC → Champion.
73. *How do you want this system to interact with the existing Line-of-Business applications?* — Integration / SC → CIO.
74. *Who will own data governance after go-live, and how will disputes be resolved?* — Governance / SC → Data Lead.
75. *What is your tolerance for deviating from Oracle/SAP best practice in exchange for matching your current process?* — Standardization / SC → Sponsor.
76. *What training and adoption effort are you prepared to fund to make this design land?* — Adoption / SC → HR / Change Lead.
77. *If we had to pick between speed and elegance on any design decision, which way would you want us to lean by default?* — Value / SC → Sponsor.

## 7. Demo and Proof (8)

78. *Before I show anything, can I confirm the three scenarios that matter most to you so I stay focused on those?* — Scoping / SC → Audience.
79. *In the scenario I just demonstrated, what would you want to do differently, and why?* — Feedback / SC → Audience.
80. *If you imagined this capability in use six months after go-live, what would be different about your week?* — Need-Payoff / SC → User Buyer.
81. *Which part of what you just saw would be hardest to get your users to adopt?* — Risk surface / SC → User Buyer.
82. *Where would this still leave you short of your ideal process?* — Gap / SC → Process Owner.
83. *If we built a focused proof of concept, what two metrics would prove to you that it works?* — POC success / SC → Sponsor.
84. *Who would need to be in the room to validate that proof of concept for you?* — Stakeholder / SC → Champion.
85. *What have you seen from other vendors that I should be aware of as we compete?* — Competitive / AE → Champion (private).

## 8. Negotiation and Close (10)

86. *Help me understand which of these commercial terms matters most to you and why?* — Open / AE → Procurement or CFO.
87. *If price were addressed to your satisfaction, what else would still stand between us and a signed contract?* — Isolation / AE → Champion.
88. *What would a reasonable payment schedule look like from your side?* — Structure / AE → CFO.
89. *Which clauses in our MSA are likely to require your legal team's attention?* — PP / AE → Legal.
90. *What does your internal approval calendar look like between now and quarter-end?* — DP / AE → Champion.
91. *If we signed on date X, when could you start? What internal work needs to start before signature?* — Urgency / AE → Champion.
92. *What would it take to get this to Board this cycle rather than next?* — Urgency / AE → Economic Buyer.
93. *If there is a price concession you would like from us, can you share what would be fair to you?* — Open price / AE → Procurement (used with silence).
94. *Is there anything we have not discussed that could still derail this in the final stretch?* — Risk surface / AE → Champion.
95. *If everything goes well from here, what is the single thing you would most want me to make sure happens on day one of implementation?* — Commitment / AE → Sponsor.

## 9. Onboarding and Adoption (10)

96. *How is the close running at month one compared to the baseline we agreed at signature?* — Baseline / CSM → Finance Director.
97. *Where are users getting stuck that we did not anticipate?* — Adoption / CSM → Process Owner.
98. *Which of the five success metrics do we have clean baseline data for today?* — Metrics hygiene / CSM → Sponsor.
99. *What has surprised you — positively or negatively — about the system since go-live?* — Open / CSM → Sponsor.
100. *Usage data shows [X percent] of users have not logged in in thirty days; what is that telling us?* — Forced-honesty / CSM → User Buyer manager.
101. *What would it take to move adoption from [current] to [target]?* — Socratic / CSM → Sponsor.
102. *Who inside your team is becoming a power user, and how can we invest in them?* — Champion-development / CSM → Department Head.
103. *Which part of the training has landed best, and which part has not landed?* — Feedback / CSM → Change Lead.
104. *What stories are you telling internally about this system — positive and negative?* — Narrative / CSM → Champion.
105. *What would we need to stop doing, start doing, or keep doing to earn a great reference from you?* — Advocacy / CSM → Sponsor.

## 10. QBR, Renewal, Expansion (15)

106. *Looking back at the success metrics we agreed at signature, where are we today?* — Accountability / CSM → Economic Buyer.
107. *What has worked better than expected, and what has worked less well?* — Open / CSM → Sponsor.
108. *What new priorities have emerged in your business since we last met?* — Discovery refresh / CSM → CxO.
109. *How is the Economic Buyer measuring the success of this investment today?* — Metrics / CSM → Sponsor.
110. *Is there anyone new in your leadership team we have not met yet who will be part of the renewal decision?* — Stakeholder / CSM → Champion.
111. *If we were to present a renewal case to the Board next quarter, what story would it tell?* — Socratic / CSM → Economic Buyer.
112. *Now that [early-phase outcome] is delivered, where does your team want to focus next?* — Expansion / CSM → CxO.
113. *What business problems are on the CFO's agenda that we have not talked about?* — Expansion / CSM → Champion.
114. *If you could point this same team at one additional problem, what would it be?* — Expansion / CSM → Sponsor.
115. *Which of our capabilities are you using least, and what would it take to get real value from them?* — Adoption / CSM → Process Owner.
116. *How is our partnership rated inside your team on a ten-point scale, and what would move it by one point?* — Advocacy / CSM → Sponsor.
117. *What would a competitor need to offer for you to seriously consider switching?* — Risk / CSM → Champion (private trust).
118. *Where is our roadmap falling short of your evolving needs?* — Roadmap / CSM → Product contact.
119. *Are there peer organizations you would like us to introduce you to?* — Community / CSM → Sponsor.
120. *If we were starting this engagement today with what you now know, what would you do differently?* — Socratic / CSM → Sponsor.

## 11. Escalation and churn-risk probes (5)

121. *Are we still the right partner for you? what is making you hesitate?* — Direct / CSM → Champion.
122. *Who inside your organization is most skeptical of the current platform, and can I meet them?* — Skeptic-surface / CSM → Champion.
123. *What would it take to rebuild trust after the [incident]?* — Repair / CSM → Sponsor.
124. *If we cannot resolve [issue] in [timeframe], what is your escalation path?* — Boundary / CSM → Champion.
125. *What is the one thing I could do this quarter that would matter most to you?* — Commitment / CSM → Sponsor.
