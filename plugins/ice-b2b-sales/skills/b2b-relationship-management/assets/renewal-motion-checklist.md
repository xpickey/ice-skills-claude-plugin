## Self-Check (Anti-Patterns)

- [ ] First renewal contact at T-180, not T-60 — **R1**
- [ ] Expansion motion separate from renewal motion — **R2**
- [ ] No reflexive discount; trade structure if discounting — **R3**
- [ ] Re-discovered stakeholders / criteria, not assumed continuity — **R4**
- [ ] Multi-year option offered to enable term ↔ discount trade
- [ ] (Thai government) e-GP / TOR workflow planned with appropriate lead time (T-270 not T-180)
- [ ] (Thai) Renewal conversation paced for relationship-led decision-making, not Western-quarterly velocity
